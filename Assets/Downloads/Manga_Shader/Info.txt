This Package contains the following:
_Materials Folder: This contains all the materials of the example mesh.
_Texture Folder: Here you find all the necessary test textures to get a good feeling for the possibilities of the shader
_Example scene: to prevue the example mesh
_Manga Shader: the power behind everything. Take you time to test it and tweak with the setting of the materials and see how I applied it to the example mesh in the scene.
_Plant Mesh: me simple mesh to test and play on with the shader.
_Rotator script: lets the model rotate in play mode

If you come across any questions please contact met at crowsfieldco@gmail.com
Its advised to not apply lightmapping to this shader. Use dynamic lighting.
